Korean Beef and Rice - Recipe

Description

With healthy ground beef, ginger, garlic, some honey, this Korean Beef Bowl recipe is perfect for serving over rice with vegetables for an easy, wholesome meal. The best part? It can be done in just 25 minutes!

Ingredients

  • cucumbers
  • garlic
  • ginger root
  • green onions (scallions)
  • lean ground beef
  • jasmine rice
  • chili-garlic sauce
  • crushed red pepper
  • honey
  • rice vinegar
  • soy sauce
  • toasted sesame oil

Steps

  1. Wash the rice
  2. Wash and cut the cucumbers and ginger and green onions
  3. Whisk together rice vingegar, honey, and crushed red pepper
  4. Peel and mince garlic and ginger
  5. Preheat skillet and add ground beef to cook
  6. Add soy sauce mixture and green onions to the pan
  7. Divide the rice, beef, and cucumbers between plates
  8. Serve and enjoy!
