Black Bean & Veggie Quesadillas - Recipe

Description

Indulge in our exquisite Black Bean and Veggie Quesadillas- a fusion of earthy black beans, saut√©ed veggies, and melted cheeses, enclosed in a golden tortilla. Served with vibrant Tomato and Avocado salsa, a medley of ripe tomatoes, diced avocados, red onions, and cilantro with a zesty lime twist. A celebration of flavors and textures in every bite.

Ingredients

  • avocados
  • black beans
  • cheddar cheese
  • cilantro
  • large flour tortillas
  • lime
  • red onion
  • tomatoes
  • black pepper & salt
  • chili powder
  • cumin, ground
  • extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Dice tomatoes, avocados, and red onions
  2. Drizzle extra virgin olive oil, add lime juice, and salt and black pepper
  3. Wash, seed, and dice red bell peppers
  4. Rinse beans and add them to the skillet with cilantro
  5. Add chili powder, cumin, salt, and black pepper
  6. Layer bean mixture and cheese layers in tortillas
  7. Cook quesadillas until golden brown
  8. Serve and enjoy!
